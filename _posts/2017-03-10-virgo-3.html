--- layout: post title: Virgo 3.7.0 Released date: '2017-03-10T19:48:00.004+01:00' author: Giamma tags: - Tomcat - Virgo - OSGi - Eclipse - Spring - Virgo Tools modified_time: '2017-03-10T19:49:55.528+01:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-7876506089821014047.post-7189760810260402569 blogger_orig_url: https://devshards.blogspot.com/2017/03/virgo-3.html ---  Today Florian announced the availability of Virgo 3.7.0. 

Virgo 3.7.0 brings several improvements (most notably Tomcat 8.5 and Spring Framework 4.2) and is paired by release 1.5.0 of the Virgo Tools, the Eclipse IDE integration.

This is the first official Virgo release since I joined the project as a committer, and it's also my first open source release in general. I am very happy and very proud of what we achieved.
